Francesca Brierley - aka heka - is a multi-disciplinary artist, songwriter and producer from Florence, now based in London.
Deeply intimate and awash with melancholia, her style of lo-fi warmth incorporates snippets of conversations previously buried deep within the notes of her phone, blending murmurings of life with themes of love and loss, nostalgia and the everyday.

Rejecting genre constraints in favour of a sonic hybridity, 2021 saw her release (a), a collection of self-produced, butchered folk songs which, through the melding of electronic and organic tones, delivered an EP that is as haunting as it is enticing. 

Having toured the UK with the cult acclaimed White Flowers, as well as playing shows with the likes of Modern Woman, Broadside Hacks and Sean Nicholas Savage, heka is now organising her first EU tour with Spang Sisters, off the back of having been handpicked by BC,NR to play Sonic City Festival in Belgium in November.

Her latest releases include: no one, a single for Open Tab, the new imprint by NY label FireTalk, and High Tide, a collaboration with London producer GG Skips (Sorry, Glows), featured on the ‘21 Slow Dance Compilation.
